Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2025

RunPace – Speed Converter ("the app") is a simple utility for converting running pace to speed and back. This policy explains what data we collect — which is none.

No Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. It does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or any form of tracking.

Local Storage Only

Your preferred unit (MPH or KM/H) and conversion direction are saved locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults API. This data never leaves your device.

No Network Access

The app does not make any network requests. No data is sent to any server, including our own.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
