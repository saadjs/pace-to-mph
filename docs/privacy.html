Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

RunPace ("the app") is a utility for converting running pace to speed, calculating race finish times, and planning split strategies. This policy explains what data we collect — which is none.

No Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. It does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or any form of tracking.

Local Storage Only

The following data is saved locally on your device using Apple's standard storage APIs and never leaves your device:

  • Your preferred unit (MPH or KM/H) and conversion direction
  • Pinned favorite conversions
  • Cached running workout details imported from Apple Health, including date, duration, distance, and source name

Apple Health Access

If you choose to enable Run History, the app requests read-only access to Apple Health. It reads only your running workouts and the distance recorded with them — no heart rate, sleep, weight, location, or any other health data is accessed. The app never writes anything to Apple Health.

You can grant, review, or revoke this permission at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → RunPace.

No Network Access

The app does not make any network requests. No data is sent to any server, including our own.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
