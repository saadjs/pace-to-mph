Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert pace to speed?

Make sure the toggle at the top is set to Pace → Speed, then type your pace in mm:ss format (e.g. 7:30) and your speed will appear instantly.

How do I switch between miles and kilometers?

Use the MPH / KM/H toggle in the control panel at the bottom. Your input will automatically be converted to the new unit.

Where is the reference table?

Tap the table icon in the top-right corner to open a full reference chart of common paces and their equivalent speeds in both MPH and KM/H.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. RunPace works entirely offline. No account or internet connection is ever required.
